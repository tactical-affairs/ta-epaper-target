description: Bill of materials cost estimates at prototype and 100-unit scale, retail pricing strategy, and competitive positioning
type: project
---

BOM estimated at $190-250 (prototype) and $120-145 (100 units). Assembled COGS ~$145-180 at 100 units.

**Why:** Need to price the product competitively between phone-based systems ($150-250) and projector systems ($1200+). Target retail is $349-449 at low volume, $299 viable at 1000+ units.

**How to apply:** Design decisions should favor cost-effective components (Pi 4 2GB over Pi 5, generic HDMI LCD, ELP/Arducam cameras). A software-only SKU ($49-79, user supplies Pi + monitor) is worth considering as a market entry strategy. Biggest competitive differentiator is standalone operation with precise shot placement on realistic paper target images -- no phone or subscription required.
